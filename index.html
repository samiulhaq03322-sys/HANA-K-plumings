The Story of Hana K

Over a Decade of Excellence

Hana K Plumbing & Gas was founded in 2011 with a simple goal: to provide a friendly, non-intimidating, and highly professional service to the South Manchester community.

As a female-led business, we bring a meticulous eye for detail and a commitment to leaving every home cleaner than we found it. We understand that having tradespeople in your home can be stressful, which is why we prioritize clear communication and respect for your property.
